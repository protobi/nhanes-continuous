National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

1999-2000 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Respiratory Health (RDQ)

Data File: RDQ.xpt

First Published: June 2002
Last Revised: NA

Component Description

 Respiratory disease section (variable name prefix RDQ) provides interview data on respiratory symptoms that may be related to allergies, chronic bronchitis or asthma.

Topics

  • Cough symptoms in children (1– 11 years)
  • Chronic bronchitis symptoms (cough and phlegm) in adults
  • Wheeze/Asthma trigger factors, severity, medication use, hospitalization

Eligible Sample

All survey participants ages 1 year of age and older were asked a subset of these questions.

Analytic Notes

Additional questions on asthma are asked in the Medical Conditions (MCQ) section of this questionnaire.
