National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

2003-2004 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Alcohol Use (ALQ_C)

Data File: ALQ_C.xpt

First Published: December 2006
Last Revised: NA

Component Description

This section on alcohol use (ALQ) focused on lifetime and current use (past 12 months). Questions are not specific to type of alcohol used.

Eligible Sample

20+ years.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

MEC Interview Room, Mobile examination Center. Interviews conducted in English and Spanish.

Quality Assurance & Quality Control

Data collections software included consistency checks, including range checks, valid response checks, and checks for logical relationships. Soft edits alerted respondents to a potential error or problems, but allowed the respondents to proceed after confirming that the entry was correct. Hard edits prohibited the respondents from continuing with the ACASI until the error was corrected.

Data Processing and Editing

Data for participants aged 12-19 years are not included in this release due to confidentiality concerns. However special use data files for this age group are available through the NCHS Research Data Center.
