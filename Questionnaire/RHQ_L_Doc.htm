National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

August 2021-August 2023 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Reproductive Health (RHQ_L)

Data File: RHQ_L.xpt

First Published: September 2024
Last Revised: NA

Component Description

The reproductive health questionnaire (variable name prefix RHQ) contains questions for females only. Questions on reproductive health include menstrual history, pregnancy history, current breastfeeding, and other reproductive conditions.

Eligible Sample

 Female participants aged 12 years and older were eligible. 

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

These questions were asked at the Mobile Examination Center (MEC) during the MEC Interview using the Audio Computer-Assisted Self-Interview (ACASI) system, in English and Spanish only. The interview was not administered to proxy informants due to the sensitive nature of the questions. In previous NHANES cycles, reproductive health questions were asked face-to-face using the Computer Assisted Personal Interview system.

Quality Assurance & Quality Control

The ACASI system is programmed with built-in consistency checks to reduce data entry errors. ACASI also uses online help screens to assist interviewers in defining key terms used in the questionnaire.

Data Processing and Editing

 Edits were made to ensure the completeness, consistency, and analytic usefulness of the data.

Analytic Notes

This public use data file excludes select variables related to pregnancy and hysterectomy for females aged 12-19 and older than 44 years due to disclosure risks. A data file with all reproductive health related variables for all females 12 years and older is available in the NCHS Research Data Center.

Several questions were dropped in NHANES August 2021-August 2023 and are not included in this data file: RHQ.018, RHQ.020, RHQ.070, RHQ.071, RHQ.073A—RHQ.073N, RHQ.074, RHQ.076, RHQ.160, RHQ.162, RHQ.171, RHQ.172, RHQ.180, RHQ.190, RHQ.197, RHQ.421, RHQ.540,RHQ.542A—RHQ542D, RHQ.554. RHQ.570.

RHD143: Current pregnancy status from question: “are you pregnant now?” Please note that these responses may differ from the pregnancy status variable RIDEXPRG in the DEMO data file.

Please refer to the NHANES Analytic Guidelines and the on-line NHANES Tutorial for further details on the use of sample weights and other analytic issues.
